- **Voice:** behind-the-scenes, proof-of-work, technical
- **Visual style:** screen captures, pipeline diagrams, real metrics on screen
- **Emoji policy:** minimal

Every clip, title, caption, and thumbnail must read as a ClippingFactoryMBM asset, not a generic MBM clip.
