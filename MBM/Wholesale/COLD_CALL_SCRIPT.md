# Cold Calling Script — Pre-Foreclosure Wholesale

## Before You Call
- Google the address to see what it looks like
- Know: appraised value, lien amount, equity, auction date
- Have your buyer ready (AMBITION, ELLIS, All Wholesale, etc.)
- Have the assignment contract ready (see template)

---

## Script #1 — Owner Occupied (Velma White, McGuire, Rodriguez)

**Ring...**

"Hi, is this [Owner Name]?"

(YES)
"Hey [Name], my name is Mohammed. I'm reaching out because I see your property at [Address] has an upcoming foreclosure auction on August 4th. Are you aware of that?"

**If they know:** "I understand — it's a tough spot. The good news is I work with investors who can buy the property before the auction, cash, as-is. You'd walk away with something instead of losing it to the bank. Would you be open to a conversation about that?"

**If they DON'T know:** "I'm sorry to be the one to tell you. But here's the thing — you still have options. Before the auction date, I can connect you with a cash buyer who'll take the property off your hands. No repairs, no realtor fees, no waiting. You'd get cash at closing. Is that something you'd want to hear more about?"

**Objection: "I'm not interested"**
"I understand completely. Just so you know, once the auction happens, the bank takes everything and you get nothing — and you could still be on the hook for the deficiency. This way you at least get something. If you change your mind, my number is [your number]."

**Objection: "How does this work?"**
"Simple. I bring you a cash buyer. We sign a contract with you at a price we both agree on. The buyer closes. You get cash. The bank gets paid. Everyone wins. I handle all the paperwork, no cost to you."

**Close:**
"If I can get a cash offer to you by tomorrow, would you be willing to look at it?"

## Script #2 — LLC / Business Owner (Harmon Property Services)

"Hi, is Carrie available?"

(Carrie speaking)
"Hey Carrie, it's Mohammed. I see Harmon Property Services has a couple of properties in foreclosure — 3134 Arizona Ave and 1510 Glen Ave. August 4th auction. Are you working on saving those or are you looking to exit?"

**Exit route:** "I've got cash buyers ready to take those off your hands. Quick close, as-is. What would make sense for you numbers-wise?"

**Save route:** "I can also bring in private money to reinstate the loans, or do a short sale. Either way, I'd rather see you come out ahead than lose it at the courthouse steps."
