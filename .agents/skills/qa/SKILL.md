# qa

**Goal:** Audit deliverables (specifically lead packs).
**Action:** 
- Programmatically verify zero duplicate leads.
- Spot-check data to ensure 100% evidence-backed signals.
- Ensure the deliverable meets the BAGA KPI: "Send me another batch."
**Rule:** The QA skill acts as the final gatekeeper. If the pack is sloppy, kick it back to the pipeline. Never deliver unverified data.
