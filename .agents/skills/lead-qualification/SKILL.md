# lead-qualification

**Goal:** Verify contact information and motivation.
**Action:** 
- Cross-reference raw data sources (e.g., matching property owners to verifiable phone numbers/emails).
- Eliminate duds, dead-ends, or non-motivated targets.
- Format the final, high-confidence CSV file.
**Rule:** If you cannot verify the contact method or the motivation signal, do not include it in the final pack.
